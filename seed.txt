Write a story about the city of Vancouver, British Columbia, where the political climate is very similar to what it is today, but the insurance industry is run by the state. So the big change that this makes is that since all property crime and vandalism is paid for by an insurance company run by the state, which is to say the province, they now have an incentive to reduce those costs by increased support of education and social services. The story should show how this impacts daily life for characters living in the city of Vancouver.  Present day residents who live with the fear of their cars being broken into and their bicycles being stolen and business owners in the true present day who live with the threat of constant graffiti and vandalism should be able to relate to the characters in the story and see how their lives are affected by this change in how the insurance industry operates.
